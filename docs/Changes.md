## Stuff that was changed from the Original:
(Some of the original version of textures and translations are available through a built-in resource pack)

#### Features:
* Raw Ore exits now (Consistency with Vanilla)
* Energy & Fluid Storages, Providers & Consumers will be able to connect to each other just like Cables/Pipes can (I implemented it this way & changing it would be a pain)
* Features that already have *"replacements"* (Example: old Particle Accelerator) will not be implemented ... yet (maybe we'll do it once the more important stuff is working)
* Removed tooltip of Tantalum and Lanthanum (they were refences to the fact that the name they had in the original was not their actual element name, but in the port that's not the case, so they were removed)

#### Textures:
* Industrial Copper -> Standard Minecraft Copper
* Ores: backround uses the new Stone/Netherrack/Endstone Texture
* Buckets: uses the new Bucket Texture

#### English Translation:
* Industrial Grade Copper -> Copper (Minecraft's default copper replaces Industrial Grade Copper)
* Minecraft Grade Copper -> Red Copper (ingot and material name) (Consistency with other Red Copper Items)
* Combine Steel -> CMB Steel (material name) (Consistency with CMB Steel Items)
* Basalt -> Volcanic Basalt (Basalt Already Exists in modern Vanilla)
* Lanthanium -> Lanthanum (That's ist actual real live name, see [Element 57](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lanthanum))
* Ingot of "resource" -> "resource" Ingot (Consistency with Vanilla)
* Block of "resource" -> "resource" Block (Consistency with Vanilla)
* Powder of "resource" -> "resource" Powder (Consistency with Ingot and Block Names)
* Bar of "resource" -> "resource" Bar (Consistency with Ingot and Block Names)
* Reinforced Block of Desh -> Desh Block (Consistency with other Block Names)
* Reinforced Block of High-Speed Steel -> High-Speed Steel Block (Consistency with other Block Names)
* Blast Furnace -> Alloying Furnace (Blast Furnace already Exists in modern Vanilla)
* HE (HBM Energy) -> NE (NuclearTech Energy) (Consistency with Mod name)
* My very own bottle opener. -> His very own bottle opener. (Because of the item name "HBM's Own Self-Made Bottle Opener", he didn't make this port after all)
* Dr>Breens Private Reserve -> Dr. Breens Private Reserve (I'm pretty sure that '>' was a typo)
* NTM Hotkeys -> NTM-Modernized Keybinds (Consistency with Mod name)
* Worker's Alloy Tool -> Desh Tool (Consistency with Desh items, where it is also just called Desh)
* Actinium Powder -> Actinium-227 Powder (Consistency with other Actinium-227 items)
* Tiny Pile of Actinium Powder -> Tiny Pile of Actinium-227 Powder (Consistency with other Actinium-227 items)
* "resource_short"Be Billet (example: Po210Be Billet) -> "resource" Be Billet (example: Polonium-210 Be Billet)
